Test Pilot Success & Achievements

To refine our platform, Sharebeez conducted multiple test pilot programs in at least three cities, proving the effectiveness of our community-driven model.

Pilot 1: Group Buying for Non-Perishable Goods

Location: North Carolina

Participants: 75+ households

Success:
• Buyers saved 20-35% on bulk purchases of home essentials, electronics, and tools.
• Vendors saw a 30% increase in pre-orders, reducing unsold stock and storage costs.

Pilot 2: Community Marketplace for Excess Goods & Tools

Location: Multiple neighborhoods

Participants: 100+ sellers and buyers

Success:
• Individuals successfully sold excess household items, unused tools, and services within their community.
• Businesses cleared out overstock inventory without relying on traditional discounting.
• Increased reuse and shared ownership, promoting sustainability.

Pilot 3: Shared Delivery & Logistics

Location: Regional pilot program

Participants: 50+ community members acting as local couriers

Success:
• Reduced delivery costs by 40% compared to commercial couriers.
• 85% of deliveries were completed within 24 hours by community members using their regular travel routes.
• Participants earned discounts and perks for making deliveries.

About Us

Affirm Technologies LLC is a North Carolina-registered technology company developing Sharebeez, an innovative platform designed to help communities save money, share resources, and support local businesses.

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses by enabling group buying, resource-sharing, and community-driven deliveries for non-perishable goods, excess inventory, tools, and services. Whether you want to buy in bulk, sell unused items, or share equipment, Sharebeez provides a seamless digital marketplace for cost-effective, community-driven commerce.

As we continue to expand, our beta app – tested in at least three cities – is evolving with new features to enhance the community marketplace experience:

  • Advanced Group Payment Options – Allowing multiple users to contribute seamlessly to group purchases.
  • Enhanced Community-Driven Delivery System – Integrating rewards, discounts, and perks for individuals making deliveries along their regular routes.
  • Vendor & Manufacturer Partnerships – Expanding partnerships with local vendors, Wise Yogi, and Anthea to bring high-quality, ethical, and sustainable products to the platform.
  • Smart Resource Sharing – Streamlining the ability to rent or borrow tools, equipment, and professional services.
  • Improved Sustainability Features – Encouraging the use of eco-friendly packaging and reducing unnecessary waste.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What can I buy or sell on Sharebeez?

A: You can buy or sell non-perishable goods, excess inventory, tools, and services.

Q2: How does group buying work?

A: Group buying allows users to pool their purchases to access bulk discounts from vendors.

Q3: How do I join a community delivery?

A: Once you sign up, you can volunteer as a local courier to help with community-driven deliveries.

Q4: What payment methods are supported?

A: We support various payment options including credit/debit cards and digital wallets.
